Privacy Guarantee

Your chat data never leaves your device. All parsing and analysis happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We have zero access to your conversations.

How to Export WhatsApp Chats

  1. Open the WhatsApp chat you want to analyze
  2. Tap the three dots menu (⋮) → More → Export chat
  3. Choose "Without media" for faster analysis
  4. Save the .txt file and upload it here
